Tokenly Web Style Guide

Hello, fellow Tokener. Use this style guide as a basis for building Tokenly web applications.

Using a visually consistent color pallete and styling makes it easier for our users to navigate across the Tokenly suite of applications. It also strengthens our brand identity and can speed-up product development.

Remember, this is a guide and not a rulebook. Style, tweak and customize per your discretion.
